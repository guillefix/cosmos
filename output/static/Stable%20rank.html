<p>The <strong>stable rank</strong> of a matrix is the ratio of its <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Frobenius%20norm">Frobenius norm</a> squared, and its <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Spectral%20norm">Spectral norm</a> squared. Note that it is at most its (linear algebraic) <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Matrix%20rank">rank</a></p>
