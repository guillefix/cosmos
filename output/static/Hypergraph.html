There are results in Probabilistic combinatorics about the number of hyperedges of a given size kkk (assume they all have the same size, such a hypergraph being called kkk-uniform) such that all hypergraphs with less hyperedges than that are guaranteed to be 2-colorable. For hypergraphs with more hyperedges than this number, there exists at least one which is not 2-colorable
