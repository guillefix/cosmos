I was trying to find an algo rithm for the discovery of the &quot;best&quot; grammar for a given set of acceptable sentences. One of the things sought for: Given a set of positive cases of ac ceptable sentences and several grammars, any of which is able to generate all of the sentences what goodness of fit criterion should be used? It is clear that the &quot;Ad-hoc grammar that lists all of the sentences in the corpus, fits perfectly. The &quot;promiscuous grammar&quot; that accepts any conceivable sentence also fits perfectly.
