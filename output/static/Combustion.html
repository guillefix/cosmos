<p>Combustion is a high-temperature exothermic redox chemical reaction between a fuel and an oxidant, usually atmospheric oxygen, that produces oxidized, often gaseous products, in a mixture termed as smoke. It often is accompanied with a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Flame">Flame</a>.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Combustion" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Combustion</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/education/guides/zvvwxnb/revision/1" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">http://www.bbc.co.uk/education/guides/zvvwxnb/revision/1</a> <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/education/guides/z6xbkqt/revision" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">http://www.bbc.co.uk/education/guides/z6xbkqt/revision</a></p>
