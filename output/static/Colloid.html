(IUPAC)</p><p>A colloidal dispersion is a system in which particles of colloidal size of any nature (e.g. solid, liquid or gas) are <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Dispersion%2520(Chemistry).html">dispersed</a> in a continuous phase of a different composition (or state).
