A measurable function between two sets XXX and YYY, belonging to Measurable spaces (X,A)(X, A)(X,A), and (Y,B)(Y, B)(Y,B), is {a Function f:X→Yf: X \rightarrow Yf:X→Y, s.t. for any E∈BE \in BE∈B, the Preimage of EEE is in AAA}. I.e. the preimage of any set in the Sigma-algebra of the co-domain is in the Sigma-algebra of the domain.
