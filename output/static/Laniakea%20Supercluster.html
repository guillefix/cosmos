<p>The <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laniakea_Supercluster" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Laniakea Supercluster</a> (Laniakea; also called Local Supercluster or Local SCl) is the <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Galaxy%20supercluster">Galaxy supercluster</a> that is home to the Milky Way and 100,000 other nearby galaxies.</p>
