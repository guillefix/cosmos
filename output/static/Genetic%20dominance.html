</div> </div> <div class="tc-tiddler-body tc-reveal"><p>Phenotype 1 is <strong>dominant</strong> to over phenotype 2 (coded by the same <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Gene.html">Gene</a>, or set of genes), if a hybrid having an allele for each, expresses phenotype 1. Phenotype 2 is then called <strong>recessive</strong>. See <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H6gwYdyn7II#t=3m54s" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">video</a>. This is called <strong>total dominance</strong>, or <strong>full penetrance</strong>.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Dominance_(genetics)" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Wiki</a> (it talks about it being a property of gealleles, but it's a property of phenotypes!, see <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H6gwYdyn7II#t=5m" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">here</a>)</p><p>Pleiotropic genes. May have both dominant and recessive traits, see <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H6gwYdyn7II#t=5m44s" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">video</a>. Sickle cell anemia is caused by a particular mutation in the gene of the <span><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math><semantics><mrow><mi>β</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">\beta</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="strut" style="height:0.69444em;"></span><span class="strut bottom" style="height:0.8888799999999999em;vertical-align:-0.19444em;"></span><span class="base textstyle uncramped"><span class="mord mathit" style="margin-right:0.05278em;">β</span></span></span></span></span> globin protein of hemoglobin. That mutation gives rise to sickle cell anemia, which is a recessive trait, but it gives rise to sickle cells, which is a dominant trait. See <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H6gwYdyn7II#t=6m07.5s" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">here</a></p><p>These properties as well as the <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Locus%2520(Genetics).html">Locus (Genetics)</a>, give rise to different <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Mode%2520of%2520inheritance.html">modes of inheritance</a>.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://biomed.brown.edu/Courses/BIO48/5.Geno.Pheno.HTML" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">PHENOTYPE AND GENOTYPE</a></p><h3 class="">A full understanding of genetic dominance requires looking at the <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Genotype-phenotype%2520map.html">Genotype-phenotype map</a>, which often requires <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Systems%2520biology.html">Systems biology</a> approaches.</h3><h3 class=""><u>Codominance</u></h3><h3 class=""><u>Partial dominance</u></h3><p>Or <strong>incomplete penetrance</strong>. See <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-1GVPjWBFqQ#t=12m" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">video</a></p><p><u>Incomplete dominance</u></p><h3 class=""><u>Pseudodominance</u></h3><h2 class=""><u>Molecular basis of dominance</u></h2><p>If you are <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Heterozygote.html">Heterozygote</a>, and one of the alleles gives rise to an <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Enzyme.html">Enzyme</a> that doesn't work, that may give rise to a recessive or a dominant trait.</p><p>If the enzymes don't interact, the ineffective enzyme won't change the phenotype (unless that enzyme is the rate limiting effect, and then the pathway will go more slowly). This is essentially a recessive trait. See <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hf3G4eD23k0#t=7m46s" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">video</a></p><p>If the ineffective enzymes interact with and negatively affect the other enzymes, it virtually stop the pathway, resulting in a <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?time_continue=528&amp;v=hf3G4eD23k0#t=8m48s" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Dominant negative trait</a>. </p></div> </div> </p> </section> </body> </html>
