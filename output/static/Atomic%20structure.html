<p><em>Structure of the periodic table</em></p><p>The periodic table is mostly determines by the electronic structure of atoms (see <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Atomic%20physics">Atomic physics</a>). See also <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Chemistry">Chemistry</a></p><p>There are three rules of thumb, which were discovered phenomenologically (I think), but are justifiable from quantum mechanics:</p><p><strong>Aufbau principle</strong>: Shells should be filled starting with the lowest available energy state. and when two shells have the same value of <span><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math><semantics><mrow><mi>n</mi><mo>+</mo><mi>l</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">n + l</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="strut" style="height:0.69444em;"></span><span class="strut bottom" style="height:0.77777em;vertical-align:-0.08333em;"></span><span class="base textstyle uncramped"><span class="mord mathit">n</span><span class="mbin">+</span><span class="mord mathit" style="margin-right:0.01968em;">l</span></span></span></span></span>, fill the one with the smaller <span><span class="katex"><span class="katex-mathml"><math><semantics><mrow><mi>n</mi></mrow><annotation encoding="application/x-tex">n</annotation></semantics></math></span><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="strut" style="height:0.43056em;"></span><span class="strut bottom" style="height:0.43056em;vertical-align:0em;"></span><span class="base textstyle uncramped"><span class="mord mathit">n</span></span></span></span></span> first.</p><p><img src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/95/Klechkovski_rule.svg/330px-Klechkovski_rule.svg.png"> (Mandelung's rule)</p><p><strong><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Hund's%20rules">Hund's rules</a></strong></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://pubs.acs.org/doi/abs/10.1021/ed5009409" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Teaching Atomic Structure: Madelung’s and Hund’s Rules in One Chart</a></p><p>The <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Atomic%20orbital">Atomic orbital</a>s are organized in <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Electron%20shell">Electron shell</a>s, and subshells.
