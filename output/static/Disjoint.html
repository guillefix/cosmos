<p>Two <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Set">Set</a>s are <strong>disjoint</strong> if they have no elements in common, that is, their <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Set%20intersection">Set intersection</a> is <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Empty%20set">empty</a>. </p><p>A collection of sets is <strong>mutually disjoint</strong> if any pair of sets in the collection is disjoint.</p>
