href="Abstract%2520algebra.html">algebraic</a> <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Invariant.html">Invariant</a>s of <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Topological%2520space.html">Topological space</a>s. This is because algebraic structures are easier to compare, so it helps for comparing topological spaces.</p><p>The notion of equivalence we focus to find invariants is based on notions of equivalence for loops in the topological space. In particular two: <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Homotopy.html">homotopic</a> equivalence, and <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Homology.html">homologic</a> equivalence.</p><h2 class=""><u><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Homotopy.html">Homotopy</a></u></h2><p>Loops are maps (<a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Continuous%2520path.html">Continuous path</a>s). Loops are equivalent when there's a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Homotopy.html">continuous deformation</a> between them.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Homotopy%2520equivalent.html">Homotopy equivalent</a></p><p><u><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Homotopy%2520group.html">Homotopy group</a>s</u></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Fundamental%2520group.html">Fundamental group</a></p><p>One problem is that they are hard to compute. All the homotopy groups are not known even for the sphere!</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Simplicial%2520approximation%2520theorem.html">Simplicial approximation theorem</a></p><h2 class=""><u><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Homology.html">Homology</a></u></h2><p>Loops are subspaces (the images of <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Continuous%2520path.html">Continuous path</a>s for e.g.) Loops are considered equivalent if they bound a surface between them, basically.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Homological%2520algebra.html">Homological algebra</a>, <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Homology%2520long%2520exact%2520sequence.html">Homology long exact sequence</a></p><h2 class=""><u>Operations on spaces</u></h2><p>Some common operations on <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Topological%2520space.html">Topological space</a>s used in topology and particularly, algebraic topology.
