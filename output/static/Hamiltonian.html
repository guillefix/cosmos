<p>A quantity that is conserved due to the <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Time%20invariance">Time invariance</a> of a physical system described with a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Lagrangian">Lagrangian</a>. It is most often equal to the total <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Energy">Energy</a> of the system. It also gives rise to a formulation of <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Classical%20mechanics">Classical mechanics</a>, and a natural way of doing <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Quantization">Quantization</a>.</p>
