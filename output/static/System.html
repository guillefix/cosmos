<p>A <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Set">Set</a> of components interacting (<a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Communication">communicating</a>) in some ways.</p><p>If their interactions affect the behaviour of the system, it is known as a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Complex%20systems">complex system</a>.</p><p>A system of <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Biology">biological</a> parts is a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Biological%20system">Biological system</a></p><p>There are of course many many more examples of systems. In fact a &quot;system&quot; is more of a perspective through which one can look at something, that allows one to organize its study. This is the aim of the <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Systems%20science">Systems science</a>s.</p>
