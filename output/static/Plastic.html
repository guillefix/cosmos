<p><strong>Plastic</strong> is a generic term used in the case of polymeric material that may contain other substances to improve performance and/or reduce costs.</p><p>Note 1: The use of this term instead of polymer is a source of confusion and thus is not recommended.</p><p>Note 2: This term is used in polymer engineering for materials often compounded that can be processed by flow.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plastic" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plastic</a></p>
