<p>Running the solution through a medium composed of porous beads. Large molecules effuse faster than small ones, because small ones are able to flow into the pores of the beads.. It is a bit counter-intuitive, because it is the opposite effect as that in <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Gel%20electrophoresis">Gel electrophoresis</a>..</p>
