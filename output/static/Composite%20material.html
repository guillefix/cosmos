<p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Composite_material" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Composite material</a> is a material made from two or more constituent materials with significantly different physical or chemical properties.</p><p>A material with constituents having different chemical composition is known as a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Mixture">Mixture</a>.</p><p>A material with constituents in different physical phases, is known as a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Dispersion%20(Chemistry)">dispersion</a>.</p><p>A material can be both a dispersion and a mixture.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Textile">Textile</a></p>
