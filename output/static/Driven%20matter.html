<p><strong>Driven matter</strong> refers to a type of <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Bulk%20matter">bulk matter</a>, often <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Soft%20matter%20physics">soft condensed matter</a>, to which energy is being applied in a way that significantly affects some of its degrees of freedom. It is thus a driven system, in the sense of <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Control%20theory%20and%20control%20systems">Control theory and control systems</a>. It is closely related to <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Active%20matter">Active matter</a>.</p>
