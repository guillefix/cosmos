<p><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Percolation">Percolation</a> on a directed <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Network%20science">Network</a>. Most of the time, it refers to percolation on a directed lattice where the direction of the edges is constrained to certain directions in the space. This is applied for instance to water percolating down a porous medium, where in this case gravity imposes a preferred direction.</p><p>It is in a different universality class than undirected bond percolation. It can be described by an <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Stochastic%20processes">stochastic process</a> similar to models describing <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Epidemics%20on%20networks">Epidemics on networks</a>.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Directed_percolation" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Directed_percolation</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://guava.physics.uiuc.edu/~nigel/courses/563/Essays_2013/PDF/wolin.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">http://guava.physics.uiuc.edu/~nigel/courses/563/Essays_2013/PDF/wolin.pdf</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/BF01357518" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Directed lattices: site-to-bond conversion and its uses for the percolation and dilute polymer transitions</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://journals.aps.org/pre/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevE.66.015104" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Percolation in directed scale-free networks</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://journals.aps.org/prl/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevLett.57.90" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">New universality for spatially disordered cellular automata and directed percolation</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://journals.aps.org/pre/abstract/10.1103/PhysRevE.74.036113" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Exhaustive percolation on random networks</a></p><p>See also <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Dynamical%20systems%20on%20networks">Dynamical systems on networks</a>.
