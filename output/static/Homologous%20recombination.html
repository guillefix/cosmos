<p>Homologous recombination is a type of <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Genetic%20recombination">Genetic recombination</a> in which nucleotide sequences are exchanged between two similar or identical molecules of DNA.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Homologous_recombination" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Homologous_recombination</a></p><p>It occurs, for instance, by <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Chromosomal%20crossover">Chromosomal crossover</a> during <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Meiosis">Meiosis</a></p>
