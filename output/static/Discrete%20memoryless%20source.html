<p>A <strong>discrete memoryless source</strong> is an <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Information%20source">Information source</a> which is:</p><ul><li>Memoryless. In this context it means that the random variables in the discrete stochastic process making the source are identically and independently distributed</li><li>Discrete. In this context it means that the alphabet is countable.</li></ul>
