<p><em>aka trait</em></p><p>A trait is a general aspect of physiology being shown in a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Phenotype">Phenotype</a>. So, for example, the trait being discussed in <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.stat.washington.edu/thompson/Genetics/1.3_genotypes.html" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">here</a> is the flower-color of the pea plant. The phenotype can be either red or white flower color, depending on the genotype.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Phenotypic_trait" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Phenotypic_trait</a></p>
