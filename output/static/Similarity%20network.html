A Similarity network is one that expressed how similar entities (expressed as the nodes) are. The degree of similarity being the weight of the node.

The weight matrix AijA_{ij}A​ij​​ represents level of similarity between entities iii and jjj in the network. A similarity network is almost always complete (the only deviation from completeness is from nodes that can't be compared for some reason).
