The most certain parts of Knowledge are those pertaining to what you experience.</li><li><strong>God-like perspective</strong>. The next level in certainty is about things we infer from what we experience. This is where most of Knowledge resides. This deduction uses tools like <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Epistemology">Epistemology</a>, <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Logic">Logic</a>, <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Science">Science</a>, etc. to infer our Knowledge of <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Reality">Reality</a>, or Physical World.</li></ol><p>The physical world is based purely on primary substances (concrete things of the <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Physics">physical world</a>.
