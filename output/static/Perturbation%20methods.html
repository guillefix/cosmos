PI)</p><p><strong>Perturbation methods</strong> explore the existence of a <u>small or large parameter</u> to derive systematically a precise approximation. More art than science, building experience is valuable.</p><p>There are two methods for obtaining precise approximations: numerical methods and analytical (asymptotic) methods. These are not in competition but complemen teach other. Perturbation methods work when some parameter is large or small. Numerical methods work best when all parameters are order one. Agreement between the two methods is reassuring when doing research. Perturbation methods often give more physical insight.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www0.maths.ox.ac.uk/courses/course/28754" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Course materials</a> (<a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://courses.maths.ox.ac.uk/node/171/materials" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">new link</a>), <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www0.maths.ox.ac.uk/system/files/coursematerial/2015/3128/3/PerturbationMethodsSupplementaryNotes.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">notes</a></p><p>See reading list there</p><h2 class=""><u>Mathematical foundation</u>: <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Asymptotic%2520approximation.html">Asymptotic approximation</a></h2><h1 class="">Applications</h1><h2 class=""><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Perturbation%2520methods%2520for%2520algebraic%2520equations.html">Perturbation methods for algebraic equations</a></h2><h2 class=""><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="Asymptotic%2520approximation%2520of%2520integrals.html">Asymptotic approximation of integrals</a></h2><h2 class=""><u>Perturbation methods for differential equations</u></h2><h3 class=""><u>Local analysis</u></h3><h3 class=""><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="Local%2520analysis%2520of%2520differential%2520equations.html">Local analysis of differential equations</a></h3><p>(as discussed in Bender's book Part 2.
