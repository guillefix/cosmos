<p>The distance between the <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Position">Position</a> of a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Point">Point</a> (often representing an <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Object">Object</a>), and a reference position. What this reference position is depends on the context. It may refer to initial position, to some average position, like <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Center%20of%20mass">Center of mass</a>, or to some equilibrium position.</p>
