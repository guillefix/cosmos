Chromatography (/ˌkroʊməˈtɒɡrəfi/; from Greek χρῶμα chroma which means "color" and γράφειν graphein "to write"[1]) is the collective term for a set of laboratory techniques for the separation of Mixtures.

  • Column chromatography: Affinity chromatography
  • Size-exclusion chromatography
  • Ion chromatography
    • Ion exclusion chromatography
  • Paper chromatography

Chromatography is a type of Separation process
