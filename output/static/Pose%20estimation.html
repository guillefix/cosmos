<p><strong>Pose estimation</strong> refers to estimating an objectâ€™s 3D <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Pose">Pose</a> from a set of 2D point projections (via something like a <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Camera%20transform">Camera transform</a>. This pose estimation problem is also known as extrinsic calibration, as opposed to the intrinsic calibration of internal camera pa- rameters such as focal length, which we discuss in Section 6.3. The problem of recovering pose from three correspondences, which is the minimal amount of information necessary, is known as the perspective-3-point-problem (P3P), with extensions to larger numbers of points collectively known as PnP (Haralick, Lee, Ottenberg et al. 1994; Quan and Lan 1999; Moreno-Noguer, Lepetit, and Fua 2007).</p><p>It is a particular instance of feature-based alignment.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Perspective-n-Point" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Perspective-n-Point</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://haralick.org/conferences/Analysis_and_Solutions_Perspective.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">analysis and solutions of the three point perspective pose estimation problem</a></p><p>Implementation using <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#OpenCV">OpenCV</a>: <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://github.com/supasorn/CardboardPositionalHack/blob/master/tracker/glcv3.cpp" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://github.com/supasorn/CardboardPositionalHack/blob/master/tracker/glcv3.cpp</a> can use for <a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jo1gkpodrDU" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">google cardboard 6DOF positional tracking</a></p><hr><p>See <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Camera%20callibration">Camera callibration</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.ics.uci.edu/~majumder/VC/new-lectures/cameracalib.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Nice explanation of some related things</a> like camera callibration, and depth estimation</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.wikiwand.com/en/3D_pose_estimation" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://www.wikiwand.com/en/3D_pose_estimation</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://users.ics.forth.gr/~lourakis/posest/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">http://users.ics.forth.gr/~lourakis/posest/</a></p><p><u>Using <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#RANSAC">RANSAC</a></u></p><p>PnP is prone to errors if there are outliers in the set of point correspondences.
