<p>Aluminium ore is called bauxite. The bauxite is purified to yield a white powder - aluminium oxide - from which aluminium can be extracted. The extraction is done by <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Electrolysis">Electrolysis</a>.</p><p><img src="http://a.files.bbci.co.uk/bam/live/content/z2vw6sg/large"></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/education/guides/zfsk7ty/revision/3" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">http://www.bbc.co.uk/education/guides/zfsk7ty/revision/3</a></p>
