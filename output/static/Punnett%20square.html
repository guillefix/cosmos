<p>The Punnett square is a diagram that is used to predict an outcome of a particular cross or breeding experiment. It is named after Reginald C. Punnett, who devised the approach. The diagram is used by biologists to determine the probability of an offspring having a particular genotype.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Punnett_square" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Punnett_square</a></p>
