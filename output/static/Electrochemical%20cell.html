<p>A simple electrochemical cell can be produced by dipping two different metals into an electrolyte and connecting them via wires and a voltmeter, bulb, motor, etc. The bigger the difference in <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Reactivity%20series">reactivity</a> between the two metals, the bigger the voltage produced.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Electrochemical_cell" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Electrochemical_cell</a></p>
