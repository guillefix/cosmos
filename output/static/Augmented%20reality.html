This will make at least three changes: * Several uses of computers will become easier and more accessible, so more people will do them. Things like interactive programming, media editing, education, exploration, and other things which should just be described as &quot;magic&quot;. * The real-world data will also allow much more prevalent use of computers for measuring stuff about the real world. Using computers by tipping on and constraining our vision to this little square thing while sitting all day is not a healthy thing. AR will allow more healthy use of computers.</p>
