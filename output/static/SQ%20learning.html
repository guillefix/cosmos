The fact that practically every concept class known to be eciently learnable in the Valiant model can in fact be learned from statistical queries (and thus with classi cation noise) raises the natural question of whether the two models are equivalent. We answer this question negatively in Section 7 by proving that the class of parity concepts, known to be eciently learnable in the Valiant model, cannot be eciently learned from statistical queries.
