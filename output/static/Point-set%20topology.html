Point-set topology

cosmos 2nd October 2017 at 2:57pm

I think it's synonymous with differential topology, and analytic topology

The study of topologies on Topological spaces, and related constructs like their bases, particularly, from set-theoretic grounds (like in Analysis).

Important concepts: Compactness, Connectedness, Separation axioms, Countability axioms
