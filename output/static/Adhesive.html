<p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adhesive" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Adhesives</a> is any substance applied to one surface, or both surfaces, of two separate items that binds them together and resists their separation.</p><p>Some synonyms: glue, cement, mucilage, or paste</p>
