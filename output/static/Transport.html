Transport

cosmos 11th April 2017 at 5:00pm

The movement of something from one place to another, often implying, it being carried by something.

The term, in Technology & Engineering most often refers to the transportation of people, and other goods/objects.

Some raw materials, as well as energy, which are important for most people are transported through automated networks which ensure efficient distribution of these essential goods. See distribution
