Cell transport

  • Membrane protein

Bilayer of Phospholipids. They live in a disordered gel phase (instead of a crystalline form). They are bound by Hydrophobic interactions

Cell membranes self-assemble, as Micelles do.

Permeable Hydrophobic molecules, like gases (CO2, O2). A bit permeable to small uncharged molecules, like water.

Immpermeable to Ions
