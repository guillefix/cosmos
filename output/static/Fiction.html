The general activity of imagining, creating, and building unreal worlds and things (Worldbuilding). Can be done collaboratively or not. Fantasy (in the psychological term) refers to the more fleeting kind of fiction done by an individual mind, as when daydreaming for example. Fiction here refers to a more general phenomenon which can involve a more conscious effort and even large scale project, as in world building.
