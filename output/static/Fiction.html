<p>The general activity of imagining, creating, and building unreal worlds and things (<a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-missing" href="#Worldbuilding">Worldbuilding</a>). Can be done collaboratively or not. <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Fantasy">Fantasy</a> (in the psychological term) refers to the more fleeting kind of fiction done by an individual mind, as when daydreaming for example. Fiction here refers to a more general phenomenon which can involve a more conscious effort and even large scale project, as in world building.</p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Fiction" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">https://www.wikiwand.com/en/Fiction</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Fiction%20shapes%20reality">Fiction shapes reality</a></p>
