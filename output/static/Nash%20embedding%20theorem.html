An embedding is not necessarily unique; two different realizations of a Riemannian metric are called isometries . .</p><p>See <a class="tc-tiddlylink tc-tiddlylink-resolves" href="#Whitney%20embedding%20theorem">Whitney embedding theorem</a></p>
