A ribbon refers to the mathematical object consisting of a Curve and an associated 1d unit perpendicular Vector field on it. Formally it is a pair (X,U)(X,U)(X,U), of a smooth Curve XXX together with a smoothly varying Unit Vector field U(t)U(t)U(t) perpendicular to to XXX (perpendicular to the Tangent vector of XXX). The ribbon is called simple and closed if XXX is simple and closed and if UUU and its derivatives agree at aaa and bbb.
