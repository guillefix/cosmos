<p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.technologyreview.com/s/603803/facebooks-ai-chief-machines-could-learn-common-sense-from-video/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Facebook’s AI Chief: Machines Could Learn Common Sense from Video</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://www.kaggle.com/c/youtube8m" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Google Cloud &amp; YouTube-8M Video Understanding Challenge</a></p><p><a class="tc-tiddlylink-external" href="https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.06624" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Temporal Generative Adversarial Nets</a> – The temporal generator consists of 1D deconvolutional layers and outputs a set of latent variables, each of which corresponds to a frame in the generated video, and the image generator transforms them into a video with 2D deconvolutional layers. This representation allows efficient training of the network parameters.
