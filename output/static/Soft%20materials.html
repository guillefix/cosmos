• Simple liquids (fully homogeneous/isotropic). See Fluid mechanics. The fluid may be a solution, but that only changes it chemical properties, not its physical ones.
  • Several types of complex fluids. Main types:
    • Solid–liquid (Suspensions (particularly Colloids) and Gels)
    • solid–gas (Granular materials, and Fibrous materials)
    • liquid–gas (Foams)
    • liquid–liquid (Emulsions)
  • Liquid crystals: nematic, smectic, cholesteric, columnar, etc.
  • Several polymeric materials are soft. E.g. some Plastics, Rubbers, Latex
  • Surfactants
  • Biological matter
  • Many kinds of Active matter and Driven matter.
  • Pastes, or soft glassy materials
